About Me

I'm Otavio, a 23 years old Software Developer from Porto Alegre, a city in southern Brazil. I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from UFSM (2014), but the main thing I learned in the University is how big is the world of IT and how much I still have to learn everyday in order to understand most of what is going on in this fast-changing subject.

When it comes to code, my main interests currently involve coding best practices, testing, design patterns and agile methodologies, but they are always changing and I can pretty much say that I'm interested in everything about IT.

Currently, I am a Consultant Developer at ThoughtWorks Brazil.

But life is not always about coding, right?

Being a gay guy, I'm very passionate about the LGBTQI cause and I try being active on it. I also empathize with the gender equality, black and other minorities causes.

I love traveling. I've already visited more than 30 countries and I really enjoy discovering new cultures and new customs.

In my free time, I also play computer games, like League of Legends and Hearthstone.

As you can probably notice, english is not my native language. If you see something written wrong, please don't hesitate on contacting me. :)
